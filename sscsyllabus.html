SSC REGISTRATION PROCESS

  1. Visit ssc website ssc.nic.in to open the official website.<\li>
  2. Click on "New User?Register Now", A Basic detail portal will be open. Now choose "Yes" if Aadhar is available otherwise choose "No". Now fill the basic details as per the instruction.
  3. Fill your personal details carefully with your 10th marksheet only. Mobile number and E-mail id should be personal and can be used by you for future purpose.
  4. Save your details. then you will proceed with Additional and Contact details.
  5. After filling your address click on"Next" . A declaration will be open, mark on agree, take draft print and then submit.
  6. After successful registration, an e-mail and message will be sent to you with the registration id and password.
  7. Reopen the website and login with your registration id and password to reset your password.
  8. In case of forgetting the password or registration no, click on Forget password

SSC CHSL APPLY

  1. Login with your registration id and password on ssc.nic.in.
  2. A welcome portal with all latest job vacancies will be open. Click on Apply in sub-section of ssc chsl
  3. Fill three centres of your choice and your 12th details asked on the portal.
  4. Upload your photo(20 kb ro 50kb) and signature(10 kb to 20 kb) with given sizes. Use Doc Scanner or any other app to Compress your documents otherwise use online platform like click here to compress
  5. use a simple photograph without cap or spectacles, otherwise application may get rejected.
    6. then click on Agree checkbox, futher click for preview and print your final application.
  7. if payment is required to be done, then take a print out of your payment status

Tier-II(Descriptive Paper):
  1. Tier-II paper will be a descriptive paper of 100 marks in "Pen and Paper mode". The duration of the descriptive will be for one hour(20 min extra will be given for scribe). The Paper will consist of writing Essay/Letter/Application/Precis
  2. The minimum Qualifying marks will be 33 per cent.
  3. The paper should be written in language chosen in the application form.
Tier-III(Skill Test/Typing Test):
  1. Skill test/Typing test for shortlisted candidates will be conducted on computer.
  2. Errors in the skill test will be calculated upt o 2 decimal places.
  3. Skill Test is madatory for Data Entry operator. No candidate is ezempted from appearing in the skill test.
  4. 8000 depressions per hour for Data Entry and 15000 key depressions per hour is required for data entry operator Grade'A'.
